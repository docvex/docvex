1. What this policy covers

Scope.

This policy explains how the docvex.ro website, the web application at docvex.ro/app, and the DocVex desktop application use cookies and similar browser-storage technologies (localStorage, sessionStorage, and IndexedDB). It supplements the Privacy Policy.

2. Cookies & similar storage

A short primer.

Cookies are small files a website places in your browser; localStorage, sessionStorage, and IndexedDB are related browser mechanisms for storing data on your device. DocVex primarily uses browser storage rather than cookies: the authentication session and your preferences are held in localStorage, temporary caches in sessionStorage, and (on the web app) the connection to your local project folder in IndexedDB.

Everything we store falls into two categories: essential (the Service cannot work without it) and preferences (remembering choices you made). We use no analytics, advertising, or cross-site tracking storage of any kind.

4. Third-party requests

External services a page may contact.

  • Supabase — the site and app talk to our EU-hosted backend for sign-in and data sync; the session is stored locally as described above.
  • Google Fonts — the website loads its typefaces from Google Fonts, which involves a request to Google's servers. The desktop app does not depend on this at runtime.
  • Google / Microsoft sign-in pages — if you use Google sign-in or connect a mailbox, those providers' own pages set their own cookies under their own policies.

None of these are used by DocVex for advertising or cross-site tracking.

5. Managing storage

You are in control.

You can clear DocVex's stored data at any time from your browser's site-data settings, or in the app via Account → Erase data, which signs you out everywhere and clears local storage. Because the stored items are essential or preference-only, there is no consent banner to manage — blocking the essential storage simply means you cannot stay signed in.

6. Changes & contact

Staying informed.

If we ever introduce a new category of storage — for example privacy-respecting analytics — we will update this policy first and, where required, ask for your consent. Questions: docvexteam@docvex.ro.
