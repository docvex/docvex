About

Intelligent legal workflows for modern law firms.

DocVex is a desktop-first workspace that helps legal teams draft, review, organize, and sign documents without the busywork. We pair a native, privacy-respecting app with focused AI tooling so firms spend less time on administration and more time on their clients.

Our mission

To give every firm — from solo practitioners to large practices — software that respects the confidentiality of legal work while removing the friction from everyday document operations.

How we build

Privacy by design, files kept local to your machine, and a calm, considered interface. We sweat the details so your team can move quickly with confidence.

Customers

Built for legal teams of every size.

DocVex serves law firms, in-house legal departments, and independent practitioners who need a dependable home for their documents and matters.

  • Boutique and solo practices streamlining intake to close.
  • Growing firms standardizing drafting from shared templates.
  • In-house teams keeping sensitive work confidential by default.
